Moshe Sulamy

I'm a faculty member at the School of Computer Science in Tel-Aviv Academic College.

I completed my PhD at Tel-Aviv University, under the supervision of Prof. Yehuda Afek.

My research interests are: distributed computing, multiprocessor programming, concurrent
data structures, and the integration of distributed computing with game theory and biology.
